Wizard Strength

Instructions
When you start the game, you should see a score at the top right of the page. You will click 1 of the 4 items to help your wizard reach his goal score. In the top left of the page you will see what your total score is so far. Continue to pick items till you reach the target score. If you hit the target score you win. If you go over the target score you lose.
